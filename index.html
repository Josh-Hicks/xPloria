The Village

You awaken in your small village after a wild night of adventuring. Only... you're not in your bed. A fellow villager finds you covered in blood, sleeping in his barn.

Startled, the villager yells, Aaaahhh! What kind of demon is this?

You reply:

Uhh... I'm from the other side of the village. The last thing I remember I was out clearing monsters from the village perimeter. I came upon an old man, but when I approached he faded out of sight like a cloud in the wind. I fell to the ground and woke up here...

The villager reluctantly sheaths his sword and steps back. He looks longingly toward the distant mountains and says I've heard tales of such things in my youth. Now more than ever, the creatures around these lands seem to be restless and full of hate. I fear the one who rules the mountain may have returned after all these years.

Who is this "mountain king" you speak of, you ask.

The villager looks you in the eyes and whispers, He is the destroyer of worlds, and the bringer of doom. If he has indeed returned from the dead... our village is in grave danger. Return home now . I have much work to do here.

I will find the answers I seek. If The Mountain King really has returned... I will destroy him.
